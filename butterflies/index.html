Butterflies and their lifecycle

Welcome

The purpose of this site is to provide information about butterflies throughout their life cycle, which has 4 stages:

  1. Egg
  2. Larva, or Caterpillar
  3. Pupa, or Chrysalis
  4. Adult Butterfly
If you are interested in butterflies, then fly through the navigation links near the top of each page.

From the Life Cycles Page, you can maneuver through the four stages of a butterfly's life cycle.

With diagrams, videos, tables of information and more, you are sure in the right place to learn about butterflies!
