Watching Butterflies

There are many options for butterfly vacations as well, including visits to Bed and Breakfasts, or migration sites for butterflies. Use a search engine or call your travel agent for more information.

Butterflies are cold-blooded and so require warm climates. You can find them year-round in the tropics, and areas around the equators, but only seasonally in most other areas. Summer is the safest bet as spring can come late in a given year, and autumn may arrive early. Butterflies alive when the weather changes will go into hibernation, and eggs will wait to hatch until the air is warm enough, just as we all like to stay in a warm bed on a cold morning. Attracting Butterflies

Want butterflies to come to you? Many people landscape and garden with a mind not just to putting in beautiful flowers but to attracting birds and butterflies. Just as you would put in a bird-feeder with birdseed to draw out cardinals and bluejays, if you want butterflies, you need their food source: nectar and fruit juice. Yes, there are certain flowers most likely to attract butterflies. This can be as simple as adding a good variety of flowers and testing what works and what doesn't from one year to the next, or you can make a real science of it. Photographing Butterflies

  • Butterflies are frightened by motion and noise, so if you want to get good pictures of butterflies, you will need to stay very still and quiet.
  • A camera with a good , and a "microzoom" feature to capture small objects while reducing blurriness, will be a priceless accessory.
  • Try to plan before you plan to shoot. Get familiar with the area, if you are not already, and factor in the sun and shadows before you settle in.
  • Take a lot of shots. Not all of what you shoot will come out as you hope, so the best protection against missed opportunity is to keep the shutters clicking. This easier and more affordable than ever with digital cameras.
  • Be natural. This doesn't mean going out in your birthday suit, but you may want to try to limit cosmetics such as deodorant and hairspray and perfume which will mask the scent of nectar flowers are trying to project.
  • Smell sweet. You can also try rubbing yourself with a little nectar or fruit juice to blend in and draw curious hungry butterflies even closer, but beware of drawing bees instead!

Raising Butterflies

Some teachers and parents help their children raise butterfly colonies. Commercial kits and products are available, and eggs can be ordered, or found in the wilderness. Then conditions are set to optimize the environment for the butterflies, plenty of food is provided for newly-hatched caterpillars, and the butterflies can be witnessed breaking out of their pupas. Just remember to let the butteflies go so they can live a full life in the wild, mate, and leave more eggs behind.
